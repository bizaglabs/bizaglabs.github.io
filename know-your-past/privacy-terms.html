System and Services Description:

Know Your Past is a social network connecting fans with their favourite celebrities. Know Your Past also runs a crawler software that discovers and analyzes millions of news articles, blog posts, user posts, user comments, photos and videos shared on various social network platforms and the wider internet to provide personalized news discovery experience to it’s users. Know Your Past analyzes the sentiment around a particular story and topics along with it’s popularity on social networks which is further used to rank stories. Some of the key features of Know Your Past are:

•From hundreds of thousands of topics, users can select their interests to get a personalized story feed called “News” based on celebrity selection.

•Tapping on buzz displayed with stories provides celebrity feeds.

•Clicking on the source displayed with stories provides source based feeds.

•Users can view feeds of favourite celebrities containing pictures and videos to read later on any device.

•Know Your Past users can showcase their talent to other fans of the community called as “FanWall”

•Users can ask questions which is purely at the discretion of the celebrity to respond to them.
